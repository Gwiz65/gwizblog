Welcome

Welcome to my blog.

I plan on using this space to try my hand at writing about issues I care about. Basic rights like free speech, privacy, due process and protection against unreasonable search and seizures are some of the areas I plan on focusing on. The intersection between copyright law and the First Amendment, in particular, is a area I'm very interested in.

Some of you might recognize my nickname (Gwiz ) from Techdirt , where I have been part of their community for years. I will probably be referencing them often here since they discuss many of the issues that interest me.
